What Does RESOLVE Do?

Addresses the challenge of effectively managing organizational complaints to improve Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI). Developes a streamlined platform that facilitates easy resolution, promoting a fair and accessible process for addressing concerns within the organization.
This innovative approach fosters transparency and accountability, fostering a culture of trust and collaboration.

AI-driven Trend Analysis and Secure Data Encryption

Utilizes AI to analyse complaint data and identify trends, enabling organizations to proactively address systemic issues and continuously improve their DEI efforts.
Prioritizes user privacy by implementing robust data encryption measures, safeguarding sensitive information shared during the complaint resolution process.

Auto-Escalation Levels Of Complaints

Allow users to submit complaints anonymously, ensuring confidentiality, and implement escalation levels based on the severity of the issue for appropriate and timely action.
With a transparent and structured escalation process, we guarantee that issues are swiftly resolved, fostering a culture of accountability and trust within our organization.
